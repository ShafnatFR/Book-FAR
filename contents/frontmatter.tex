    {\Large\bfseries ABSTRAK \par}
    \addcontentsline{toc}{section}{ABSTRAK}
    \vspace{0.5cm}
    {\large\bfseries Food AI Rescue \par}
    \vspace{0.5cm}
    Oleh: \\
    Shafnat Fuaini Ramadhan \\
    607012400075 \\
\end{center}
\vspace{0.5cm}
Indonesia menghadapi tantangan paradoks ketahanan pangan, di mana tingginya volume limbah makanan (\textit{food waste}) dari sektor industri kuliner terjadi bersamaan dengan masalah kerawanan pangan di tingkat komunitas. Permasalahan utama bersumber dari "keterhubungan yang terputus" (\textit{disconnected link}) antara penyedia surplus pangan dan kelompok rentan, serta kekhawatiran pelaku usaha terkait jaminan mutu makanan donasi dan risiko liabilitas. Penelitian ini bertujuan untuk merancang dan membangun platform Food AI Rescue (FAR), sebuah agregator logistik digital berbasis \textit{Progressive Web App} (\textit{PWA}) non-komersial. Sistem ini dikembangkan menggunakan \textit{framework} Node.js dan mengintegrasikan teknologi kecerdasan buatan (\textit{Computer Vision}) melalui layanan \textit{API} Gemini 3. Platform FAR memiliki tiga fungsi utama, yaitu: pencocokan distribusi logistik secara \textit{real-time} berbasis radius lokasi (\textit{AI Matching \& Allocation}), verifikasi kelayakan visual serta prediksi kehalalan makanan sebelum disalurkan (\textit{AI Quality Verification}), dan dasbor pelaporan kalkulasi pengurangan jejak karbon. Metode pengembangan difokuskan pada penyelesaian perangkat lunak yang diverifikasi melalui uji coba operasional \textit{end-to-end} (simulasi \textit{role-playing}) di lingkungan \textit{server} lokal untuk mengevaluasi kelancaran fungsionalitas aplikasi, akurasi verifikasi AI, dan akurasi \textit{database}. Hasil dari penelitian ini adalah sebuah sistem yang memberikan jaminan keamanan pangan dan transparansi pelaporan, sehingga dapat memecahkan kebuntuan keraguan pelaku usaha untuk mendonasikan makanannya. Kehadiran platform ini diharapkan mampu mengubah beban limbah pangan menjadi kemanfaatan sosial, serta mendukung pencapaian \textit{Sustainable Development Goals} (\textit{SDGs}) poin 2 (Tanpa Kelaparan) dan poin 12 (Konsumsi dan Produksi yang Bertanggung Jawab).

    {\Large\bfseries ABSTRACT \par}
    \addcontentsline{toc}{section}{ABSTRACT}
    \vspace{0.5cm}
    {\large\bfseries\itshape Food AI Rescue \par}
    \vspace{0.5cm}
    By: \\
    Shafnat Fuaini Ramadhan \\
    607012400075 \\
\end{center}
\vspace{0.5cm}
\textit{Indonesia faces the paradoxical challenge of food security, where high volumes of food waste from the culinary industry coexist with food insecurity at the community level. The main issues stem from a ``disconnected link'' between food surplus providers and vulnerable groups, as well as business operators' concerns regarding the quality assurance of donated food and liability risks. This research aims to design and build the Food AI Rescue (FAR) platform, a non-commercial digital logistics aggregator based on a Progressive Web App (PWA). This system was developed using the Node.js framework and integrates artificial intelligence (Computer Vision) technology through the Gemini 3 API service. The FAR platform has three main functions: real-time logistics distribution matching based on location radius (AI Matching \& Allocation), visual eligibility verification and halal food prediction prior to distribution (AI Quality Verification), and a reporting dashboard for carbon footprint reduction calculations. The development method focused on software validation through end-to-end operational trials (role-playing simulations) in a local server environment to evaluate the application's functional performance, AI verification accuracy, and database accuracy. The result of this research is a system that provides food safety assurance and reporting transparency, thereby resolving the hesitation of businesses to donate their food. It is hoped that this platform will transform the burden of food waste into social benefits and support the achievement of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Goal 2 (Zero Hunger) and Goal 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production).}
